Getting Started

Welcome to Go!Tweet!

Go!Tweet is the quickest and easiest way to send measurements from your LabQuest or LabQuest Mini device to Twitter.

Note: you will need to have the drivers installed for your device. If you have Logger Pro, Logger Lite, the LabQuest Emulator, or manually installed the drivers from the Vernier website, Go!Tweet will work. Otherwise, you can pick up the latest drivers from http://www.vernier.com/drivers/

To use Go!Tweet:
  1. Connect your LabQuest or LabQuest Mini to your computer.
  2. Attach a supported sensor to an analog channel on the LabQuest or LabQuest Mini.
  3. Link Go!Tweet with your Twitter Account.
  4. Tweak your preferences in the Options dialog.
  5. Watch Go!Tweet tweet your sensor values.
